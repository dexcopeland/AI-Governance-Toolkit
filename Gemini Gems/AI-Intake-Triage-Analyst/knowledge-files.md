# Knowledge files — AI Intake & Triage Analyst

Upload these to the Gem so its output matches your process and tiering.

| File | Why | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| `Templates/AI-Use-Case-Intake-Form.md` | So it extracts attributes in the same shape you collect them | **Required** |
| Your org's **AI policy / acceptable-use policy** | Defines red lines & prohibited uses for the "Prohibited" tier | **Required** |
| Your **risk-tier definitions** (if you have a formal one) | Aligns its tiering to yours | Recommended |
| Your **review-path map** (which teams review what) | So it routes to the right reviewers | Recommended |
| `NIST.AI.100-1.pdf` (NIST AI RMF) | Grounding in MAP-function context questions | Optional |

## Notes
- If you have no formal tier definitions yet, the Gem will use the four tiers in its instruction (Minimal/Limited/High/Prohibited) — capture those into a policy doc later and upload it.
- Re-upload the intake form whenever you revise it, so extraction stays aligned.
